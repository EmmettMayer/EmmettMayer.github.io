Fingerboard Tuning

Tuning on a fingerboard it what allows the trucks to move back and forth and return to their original position. They are also responsible for how the board feels while turing and how easy or difficult it is to do so.
there are two primary styles you can get, there are Bubble Bushings which are a Teak tuning product as they invented it and Bubble Bushings are by far the most popular style for people who are serious about fingerboarding. But there are also O rings, much less popular and offer a stiffer experience.
