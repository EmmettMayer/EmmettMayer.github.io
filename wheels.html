Fingerboard Wheels

Fingerboard wheels span a very wide spectrum. From wheels that are just super cheap pieces of plasitc that you bolt right on, to wheels turned on a lathe out of urethane with mini cartridge bearings in them so they can freely spin and stay straight on the trucks.
Higher end fingerboard wheels can also be poured out of resin and have a cartridge bearing put into them. Resin wheels will be harder than urethane wheels and less grippy than urethane wheels.
